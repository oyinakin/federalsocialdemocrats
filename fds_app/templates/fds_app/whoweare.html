    About FSD

    Federal Social Democrat was founded in 2020 by a conglomerate of people who believe in the need to keep Nigeria as one whilst building a better Nigeria free of corruption, bad governance and poverty.

    Vision

    To build an egalitarian Federal Union and transform Nigeria into a global economic and technological power, the pride of Africa. Core vision: Selfless Service, Zero-Tolerance for All Forms of Corruption, Justice & Equity, Knowledge-Based Society, Transparency and Accountability and Integrity

    Mission

    To galvanise the people to build a value-driven society inspired and sustained by selfless leaders, restoring trust in public service, bringing governance closer to the people, delivering the greatest good to the greatest number of people.
